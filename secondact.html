KATE

[_As if he was the stupid one._]

Poor Harry.

SIR HARRY>
And it couldn't have been going on for long, for I would have noticed the change in you.

KATE
Would you?

SIR HARRY>
I knew you so well.

KATE
You amazing man.

SIR HARRY>
So who was he? Out with it.

KATE
You are determined to know?
